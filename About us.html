Innovate U , which has operated as an RTO in Queensland for over five or six years, started this RTO institution for IT technology in 2017 to direct our training services to a specialised industry sector: IT.


Our training methodology is centred on a blended learning model that incorporates hands-on practical training through demonstration, observation and discussion. This hands-on element is complemented by an online or paper-based theory component.


What sets us apart is our ability to flexibly adapt and customise training solutions to meet your specific needs, exceeding the standards of other RTOs in Australia.


We provide training at all educational levels from school to industry.
